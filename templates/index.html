Internet User Rate

Individuals using the internet as a percentage of a country's population.
Source: World Bank

Freedom of the Press Status

Level of freedom and editorial independence enjoyed by the press: Not Free, Partly Free, and Free. Source: Freedom House

Corruption Index

Perceived levels of public sector corruption from highly corrupt (0) to very clean (100). Source: Transparency International

Polity Score

Captures regime institionalized authority on a scale ranging from fully autocratic (-10) to fully democratic (10). Source: Center for Systemic Peace - The Polity Project

Correlation Bubble Chart

Chart displays the correlation between Corruption, Internet User Rate, Political Regime, and Freedom of the Press where countries with more corrupt autoritarian regimes have lesser internet user rates. There are likely confounding variables that we are not controlling for that influence these results, i.e. country development level, education, internal conflicts, etc.
